Free Online Statistical Analysis Tool

QuickStats lets you run regression models and generate descriptive statistics directly in your browser — no installation, no sign-up, and no data uploaded to any server. Powered by R and WebAssembly, all analysis runs locally on your device.

  • Linear regression for continuous outcomes
  • Logistic regression with odds ratios for binary outcomes
  • Cox regression for survival and time-to-event data
  • Mixed-effects models for clustered or hierarchical data
  • Publication-ready Table 1 descriptive statistics
  • Forest plots, diagnostic plots, and scatter plots
  • Export to PDF, Word, or plain text

Perfect for students, researchers, and clinicians who need quick, private statistical analysis.
